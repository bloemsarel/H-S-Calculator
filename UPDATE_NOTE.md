## Formula Remains Unchanged

The actual calculation method is **exactly the same**:
- **0-10 kg**: 100 mL/kg/day
- **11-20 kg**: 1000 mL + 50 mL/kg for each kg above 10
- **>20 kg**: 1500 mL + 20 mL/kg for each kg above 20

This is the standard Holliday-Segar method that has always been used for all ages, with clinical judgment applied based on patient characteristics.

## Medical Accuracy

The Holliday-Segar method is a **validated approach** for estimating maintenance fluid requirements that:
- Was originally developed for pediatric patients
- Is commonly used across all age groups
- Requires clinical judgment for special populations
- Should be adjusted based on patient factors

### Clinical Considerations for All Ages:

**Neonates & Infants:**
- May need adjustments in first days of life
- Consider insensible losses
- Monitor carefully

**Children & Adolescents:**
- Formula works very well
- Standard of care in many institutions

**Adults (especially >70 kg):**
- Use as starting point
- Consider comorbidities
- Adjust based on clinical response
- Alternative methods may be preferred in some cases
